Information about tweaks

Tool tips

All tweaks have a tool tip which shows what it does and which *.ini values it changes.
You can hover your cursor over an option to display said tool tip.

Color-code

Some tweaks might be problematic or have side effects which is why I color-coded them:

Color Meaning
Black Nothing to inform about really. Tweak works as it should.
Blue There is a notice that might be worth reading about.
Magenta Experimental/Untested: This tweak might not do anything.
Yellow Generally usuable but might have side effects. Please read the warning.
Red Unsafe: Has severe side effects such as crashing or severe graphical glitches.

About the slider range

Sliders have a range (minimum / maximum) but for most of them you can exceed the range by manually typing a value into the number fields next to them.
